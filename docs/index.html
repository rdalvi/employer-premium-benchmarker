Notes & caveats

    How is this calculated?

    For each of single and family coverage:

    1. Start with the national average annual premium from MEPS-IC.
    2. Multiply by the state index (state premium ÷ national premium).
    3. Multiply by the firm-size factor for the employer's size bucket.
    4. Multiply by the industry factor (national-level industry premium ratio).
    5. For self-funded, multiply by the SF/FI cost ratio (~0.96), reflecting elimination of state premium tax and insurer profit margin, net of stop-loss premium.

    Single and family results are blended using the configured enrollment-tier mix. Figures shown are point estimates from a bucket-level model and do not reflect employer-specific variance from age/sex mix, claims history, or plan design.
